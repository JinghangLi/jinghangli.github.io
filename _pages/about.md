<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

<div class="lang-en">
I am a Ph.D. student at the College of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, Hunan University (HNU), advised by Prof. Yi Zhou (周易). I received my M.Eng. from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) and previously worked as a research assistant at FAST Lab, Zhejiang University (ZJU), advised by Associate Professor Fei Gao (高飞). My research focuses on 3D perception and multimodal foundation models for autonomous driving, with particular interests in event-based vision, data-centric learning, and geometry-aware modeling.

My recent work spans event-camera-based multimodal 3D perception, time-to-collision estimation, multimodal perception and planning, and scalable data engines for 3D understanding. I am dedicated to exploring the potential of multimodal large language models for applications in autonomous driving and embodied intelligence.
</div>

<div class="lang-zh">
我目前在湖南大学（HNU）人工智能与机器人学院读博，导师为周易教授。硕士毕业于北京理工大学（BIT），曾在浙江大学（ZJU）FAST Lab 担任科研助理，指导教师为高飞副教授。研究方向聚焦自动驾驶的 3D 感知与多模态基础模型，重点关注事件相机、数据驱动学习与几何建模。

近期工作涵盖基于事件相机的多模态 3D 感知、碰撞时间估计、多模态感知与规划，以及面向 3D 理解的可扩展数据引擎。我致力于探索多模态大语言模型在自动驾驶与具身智能中的应用潜力。
</div>
